Detect Cancer, Colitis, and Polyps

ColoSensus is a convolutional-neural-network (CNN) based web application trained to predict and identify features indicative of Colorectal Cancer, Ulcerative Colitis, and Colon Polyps. The CNN model was trained using the HyperKvasir, a public dataset specifically designed for disease classification tasks.
